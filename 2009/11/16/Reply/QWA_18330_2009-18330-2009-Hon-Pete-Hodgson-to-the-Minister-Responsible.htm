18330 (2009). Hon Pete Hodgson to the Minister Responsible for Ministerial Services (16 Nov 2009): How much, if anything, has Ministerial Services spent on flowers and floral arrangements for ministerial offices and residences since 1 January 2009 and how does that figure break down by Minister?
Hon John Key (Minister Responsible for Ministerial Services) replied: I am advised by Ministerial Services of the following cost of flowers and floral arrangements brought to charge for each Minister since 1 January 2009. It should be noted that this total also includes flowers purchased on an ad hoc basis for occasions such as staff farewells or bereavements.
