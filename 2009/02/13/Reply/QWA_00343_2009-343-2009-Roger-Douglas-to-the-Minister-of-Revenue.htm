343 (2009). Hon Sir Roger Douglas to the Minister of Revenue (13 Feb 2009): How many taxpayers over the age of 18 but under the age of 65 fall into each of the following categories: Married, no dependents, one income; Married, one child, one income; Married, two children, one income; Married, three children, one income; Married, four children, one income; Married, five children, one income; Married, six children, one income; Married, seven or more children, one income; Married, no dependents, two incomes; Married, one child, two incomes; Married, two children, two incomes; Married, three children, two incomes; Married, four children, two incomes; Married, five children, two incomes; Married, six children, two incomes; Married, seven or more children, two incomes; Single, no dependents; Single parent family, one child; Single parent family, two children; Single parent family, three children; Single parent family, four children; Single parent family, five children; Single parent family, six children; Single parent family, seven or more children; how many of each of these groups of taxpayers fall into the under $14,000 income bracket, the $14,001-$50,000 income bracket, the $50,001-$70,000 income bracket and the over $70,001 income bracket; and what is the total income to the IRD from taxpayers in each of these family groups broken down by these income brackets?
